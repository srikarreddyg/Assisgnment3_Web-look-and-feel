Categories

The information acquired from the survey is divided into several categories based on characteristics such as typology, economy, deals, and age. The classes are further subdivided into a variety of divisions. Every classification is given a brief ID and a representation that expresses the Class's capability. For example, the Monetary Class (Sal) is divided into classes such as $1,000,000 and up; $500,000 to $999,999, In a similar vein, different classifications have sub-classes that further characterize the data.
